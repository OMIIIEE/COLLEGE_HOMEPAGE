Birla Institute of Technology

Mesra, Ranchi - India

Birla Institute of Technology Mesra is a deemed 
university under Section 3 of the UGC Act in 
Jharkhand, India.The institute was established 
in 1955 by the leading industrialist and 
philanthropist, BM Birla and was granted 
autonomous status in 1972 under Section 30 of 
UGC Act 1956.
